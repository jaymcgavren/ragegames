The Best of the Consoles on Your Terminal

Emulators

Callus Capcom Arcade Supports a variety of Capcom arcade games including U.N. Squadron and Street Fighter II. You'll need 32 MB RAM or more for some games. Requires DOS4GW.EXE to run.
Genecyst Genesis A Genesis emulator from the author of Nesticle. Great quality but the minimum system requirements are pretty high. Requires DOS4GW.EXE to run.
M.A.M.E. Arcade This is actually dozens of emulators in one. Supports a vast number of older arcade games, and the system requirements are fairly low.
Massage Master System A reasonably fast emulator; doesn't support every game yet though.
Nesticle NES The premiere emulator for NES (or any system, in fact). Supports Net connections for two players in Win95, has a nice GUI, and includes several hacking tools and a debugger right inside. Requires DOS4GW.EXE to run.
Virtual
Game Boy		 Game Boy The first-ever console emulator and still the best for Game Boy. Runs at full speed even on slower machines.
Z26 Atari 2600 An excellent 2600 emulator that runs at full speed even on slower systems.
ZSNES SNES Wickedly fast on any system and easy to use. Definitely a must-have!
