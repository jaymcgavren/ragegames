Mushroom Dreams

That bothersome Bowser has kidnapped all your mushroom friends! You'll have to travel to eight different castles in a quest to rescue your fellow shrooms and beat Mario to the voluptuous Princess Toadstool.

Beware! Bowser's not about to let you just waltz in the front door. He's sent his evil mushrooms and other baddies to try and stop you. Don't worry, though. If you can find the magic turtle shell you'll transform into the mighty firebreathing Koopa Troopa and squash those creeps.

Will you make it to your captured comrades? Or will Bowser sautee you with a bit of butter? Only time will tell...
