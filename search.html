Sorry, but there have been some problems getting the search script set up on the separate CGI server, and for now the search form is nonfunctional. If you really have need of it, E-mail me and pester me to get it fixed. Maybe I can also help you find what you're looking for.

Simply type a text string you'd like to search for (game titles are especially effective) into the field at the left and hit Enter or click the Search button. A list of all the pages on this site that contain that string will be returned, sorted by title, with a link to each page.

Setting Boolean to And will only return documents that contain all the words in your search. Setting it to Or will return documents that contain any of your search words.

Setting Case to Insensitive means that pages containing your search words will be returned regardless of the capitalization of those words. Setting it to Sensitive means that the word must be capitalized exactly as you typed it to be found.
